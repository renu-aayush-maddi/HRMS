ATTENDANCE MODULE - PENDING FEATURES & IMPROVEMENTS

========================================
HIGH PRIORITY (IMPLEMENT SOON)
==============================

1. Attendance Regularization

Current Situation:

* Employee forgets Check-In
* Employee forgets Check-Out
* Employee punches incorrect time
* No way to correct attendance

Required Implementation:

* Employee submits regularization request
* Manager approval workflow
* HR approval workflow (optional)
* Automatically update attendance after approval

Business Importance:

* Mandatory feature in most organizations
* Prevents payroll inaccuracies
* Handles genuine employee mistakes

2. Employee Access Restriction

Current Situation:

* Any employee can view another employee's attendance records

Required Implementation:

* Employee → View only own attendance
* Manager → View team attendance
* HR/Admin → View all attendance records
* Use JWT claims for authorization

Business Importance:

* Data privacy and security
* Role-based access control

3. Double Session Protection

Current Situation:

* Employee checks in
* Does not check out
* Attempts check-in again next day
* System behavior is undefined

Required Implementation:

* Prevent new check-in if previous attendance is still open
* Or automatically close previous session
* Validate incomplete attendance records

Business Importance:

* Prevents incorrect attendance calculations
* Maintains data integrity

4. Future Date Protection

Current Situation:

* Attendance filters may allow future dates

Required Implementation:

* Prevent attendance creation for future dates
* Prevent future date searches where not applicable

Business Importance:

* Data validation and consistency

5. Attendance Status Constants

Current Situation:

* Status values are hardcoded strings

Required Implementation:

* Create centralized constants

Example:

* Present
* Absent
* Late
* HalfDay

Business Importance:

* Prevents spelling mistakes
* Easier maintenance
* Consistent status handling

========================================
MEDIUM PRIORITY (PAYROLL RELATED)
=================================

6. Working Hours Calculation

Current Situation:

* Only Check-In and Check-Out times are stored

Required Implementation:

* Calculate total working hours
* Include working hours in attendance response

Example:

* Check-In: 09:00 AM
* Check-Out: 06:00 PM
* Working Hours: 9 Hours

Business Importance:

* Required for payroll calculations
* Required for attendance reports

7. Late Arrival Logic

Current Situation:

* No late attendance detection

Required Implementation:

* Compare check-in time with office start time
* Mark attendance as Late when applicable

Example:

* Office Start Time: 09:00 AM
* Employee Check-In: 09:45 AM
* Status: Late

Business Importance:

* Attendance discipline
* Payroll deductions (if applicable)

8. Half-Day Logic

Current Situation:

* No half-day calculation

Required Implementation:

* Define minimum working hours
* Mark attendance as HalfDay when threshold is not met

Example:

* Working Hours < 4 Hours
* Status = HalfDay

Business Importance:

* Payroll processing
* Attendance policy enforcement

9. Early Checkout Logic

Current Situation:

* No validation for early checkout

Required Implementation:

* Define minimum required working hours
* Detect early checkout
* Apply payroll impact if necessary

Business Importance:

* Attendance compliance
* Payroll calculations

10. Monthly Attendance Summary

Current Situation:

* Only daily attendance records available

Required Implementation:

* Present Days
* Absent Days
* Late Days
* Half Days
* Total Working Hours

Business Importance:

* Payroll generation
* Employee reporting
* Manager reporting

MOST CRITICAL MISSING FEATURE
=============================

Attendance Regularization

Reason:
Without attendance regularization, employees have no way to correct:

* Missed Check-Ins
* Missed Check-Outs
* Incorrect Punch Times

This feature exists in almost every real-world HRMS and is essential before moving to production.
